Scene with BubbleRob. In this case it's aim is to ride on a white path
and stop for some time on lines of specific color. We can change it's
task by changing value of certain variables:

colorMode:
	- 1 -> Tracking path of black color
	- 2 -> Tracking path of white color

whichColorToStop:
	- 0 -> Stop at black line
	- 1 -> Stop at red line
	- 2 -> Stop at green line
	- 3 -> Stop at blue line
